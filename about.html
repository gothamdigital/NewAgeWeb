Based in Brooklyn, New Age Iron Works, Inc. provides high-precision steel fabrication and structural solutions for New York’s most demanding developers and contractors.

We bridge the gap between complex architectural vision and physical reality. By combining meticulous shop detailing with advanced fabrication techniques, we ensure every structural challenge is met with an elegant, code-compliant solution.

Our specialty lies in structural steel, custom stairs, and ornamental ironwork. We manage the process from initial drawings to final delivery, maintaining strict adherence to schedules while upholding the industrial quality that New Age is known for.
